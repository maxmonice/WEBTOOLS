About Us

Luke's Seafood Trading is your premier source for authentic, fresh Japanese-inspired seafood. We are an excellent quality seafood supplier, meticulously sourcing premium ingredients daily to ensure peak freshness. Our mission is to serve, supply, and cater with one goal: to share the excellence of every dish with you.

Located at Vulcan St. cor C5 Road, Taguig, we proudly offer high-quality services. We run a catering business for custom, fresh seafood dishes at any event, and a takeaway shop specializing in delicious and affordable sushi and sashimi.
